Who I Am

I am Md. Faihaj Alam Topu, a student of CSE at BUET. I am a Software Developer specializing in backend development.

In terms of programming languages, I am proficient in C, C++, Java, Python, and Golang. I have significant experience with Django, as I have completed most of my undergraduate software projects using it for both backend and full-stack development. Additionally, I have worked on backend projects using Golang. For frontend development, I generally use the React framework and Bootstrap. I am experienced in working with both SQL databases (PostgreSQL and MySQL) and NoSQL databases (MongoDB).

In addition to my technical skills, I have a strong foundation in designing scalable microservices architectures using Docker and Kubernetes, along with secure API management through Kong API Gateway. Recently, I have been exploring emerging technologies like Rust, Spring Boot, and .NET to expand my capabilities and tackle future challenges.

I am deeply curious about learning new frameworks and implementing innovative techniques. I am committed to selecting the best technology stack and delivering high-quality results for my projects.

I also love football, although I might not be very good at it. In my leisure time, I enjoy watching series and listening to audiobooks.
